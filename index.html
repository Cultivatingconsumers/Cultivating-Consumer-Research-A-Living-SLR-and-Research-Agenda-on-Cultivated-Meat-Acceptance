Living Database: Cultivating Consumer Research

Introduction

Welcome to the living database associated with the systematic literature review titled “Cultivating Consumer Research: A Living Systematic Review and Research Agenda on Cultivated Meat Acceptance”.

This database compiles relevant studies following the TCCM framework (Theory, Context, Characteristics, and Method; Paul et al., 2023). It includes primary peer-reviewed research on consumer responses to cultivated meat.

The objective of this resource is twofold:

  • To provide a centralized and comprehensive mapping of existing research in the field
  • To serve as a continuously updated resource supporting a living systematic review, reducing duplication and enabling ongoing synthesis of emerging evidence

Data Coverage and Scope

The dataset currently includes studies retrieved up to 30 June 2025.

The database is actively maintained and curated through:

  • Addition of newly identified or missing studies
  • Updating and refinement of existing entries
  • Biannual incorporation of newly published research, aligned with peer-reviewed publication cycles
